COURSES WE OFFER

Nursery

Nursery program nurtures early development through play, exploration, and care. Children are introduced to colors, sounds, and social skills in a safe, joyful environment. Its focus is pivoted on building a strong foundation for emotional and cognitive growth.

Kindergarten

Kindergarten at Academia Institute blends creativity with early academic learning. Young learners engage in structured activities that spark curiosity and encourage independence. This course prepares children for the next step with confidence and excitement.

Preparatory

The Preparatory level bridges playful learning with readiness for formal schooling. Students develop foundational literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills. This approach builds both competence and confidence for Grade School.

Grade School

Grade School offers a well-rounded curriculum in literacy, math, science, and values education. Students are guided to think independently, collaborate, and build discipline. Grade school helps shape learners into curious thinkers and responsible citizens.

Junior High School

The Junior High School program fosters academic growth and character development. Students are challenged to think critically, express themselves, and apply knowledge in real-world contexts. This program prepares them for the academic demands of Senior High.

Senior High School

Senior High School offers specialized tracks to prepare students for college or career. Whether in STEM, HUMSS, or ABM, learners gain relevant skills and deeper subject understanding. Its holistic approach ensures readiness for life beyond school.
